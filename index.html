SlimeBreaker is a squad based real time combat game about pushing through waves of slime to make it to the other side of the mountain. Explore caverns, mine crystals, and build mine tracks. Lay traps, build fortifications, and carefully position your squad to break the unending tide of slime that threatens to bury you deep within the darkness.
